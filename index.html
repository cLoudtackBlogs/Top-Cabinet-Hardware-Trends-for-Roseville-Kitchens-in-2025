Top Cabinet Hardware Trends for Roseville Kitchens in 2025

When it comes to kitchen design, the details make all the difference. Cabinet hardware—those knobs, pulls, and handles that adorn your cabinets and drawers—might seem like a small element, but they can dramatically impact your kitchen's overall look and functionality. As we move through 2025, Roseville homeowners are embracing exciting new hardware trends that elevate their kitchen designs.

At Precision Finish, we've helped countless Roseville homeowners transform their kitchens through cabinet refinishing and hardware updates. In this guide, we'll explore the top cabinet hardware trends that are making waves in Roseville kitchens this year, helping you make informed decisions for your next kitchen update.

Why Cabinet Hardware Matters

Before diving into specific trends, it's worth understanding why cabinet hardware deserves careful consideration:

Visual impact - Hardware serves as the "jewelry" of your kitchen, adding personality and style
Functional importance - The right hardware makes cabinets and drawers more comfortable and convenient to use
Cost-effective update - Changing hardware is one of the most affordable ways to refresh your kitchen's appearance
Design cohesion - Hardware ties together various elements of your kitchen design, from fixtures to appliances

Trend 1: Mixed Metals

The days of matching all metal finishes in a kitchen are behind us. Roseville homeowners are embracing the sophisticated look of thoughtfully mixed metals, creating depth and visual interest in their kitchens.

Popular mixed metal combinations include:

Brass or gold hardware paired with stainless steel appliances
Matte black hardware with brushed nickel fixtures
Copper accents combined with bronze or oil-rubbed bronze elements

Trend 2: Matte Black Hardware

Matte black hardware continues its strong presence in Roseville kitchens, offering a bold yet versatile option that works with virtually any cabinet color or kitchen style. This finish has evolved from a trend to a modern classic that adds sophisticated contrast and definition.

Matte black hardware is particularly effective:

On white or light-colored cabinets for dramatic contrast
With wood tones to create a warm, contemporary look
In transitional kitchens that blend traditional and modern elements

Trend 3: Statement Sizing

Hardware is getting bolder in 2025, with Roseville homeowners embracing oversized pulls and handles that make a dramatic statement. This trend is particularly evident in minimalist kitchens where cabinet fronts are clean and simple, allowing the hardware to take center stage.

Popular statement sizing options include:

Extra-long drawer pulls that span almost the entire width of drawers
Substantial door handles with thicker profiles and larger gripping areas
Oversized knobs that create visual impact on pantry or specialty cabinets

Hardware Selection Advice

When selecting new hardware for your Roseville kitchen, bring home samples before making a final decision. Test how the hardware feels in your hand and how it functions with your specific cabinet construction. What looks beautiful might not always be the most comfortable to use daily.

Hardware Considerations for Cabinet Refinishing Projects

If you're planning a cabinet refinishing project in your Roseville home, hardware selection should be an integral part of your planning process:

1. Consider Hardware Early

Select your hardware before finalizing your cabinet color. The metal finish and style of your hardware should complement your chosen cabinet color and overall kitchen design.

2. Address Existing Holes

If changing from knobs to pulls (or vice versa) or adjusting hardware placement, existing holes will need to be filled and refinished. Professional cabinet refinishing services like Precision Finish can seamlessly address these issues as part of the refinishing process.
